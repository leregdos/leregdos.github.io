Feeding your cat with cat food ensures they receive the proper balance of nutrients essential for their health, including taurine, which is crucial for heart and eye health and not found in adequate amounts in non-cat foods. Cat food is specifically formulated to match the dietary requirements of felines, who have unique needs different from humans and other pets.

Many cats are lactose intolerant, meaning they lack the enzyme needed to digest lactose in dairy products, leading to digestive upset, diarrhea, and discomfort. Dairy products, contrary to popular belief, do not provide any essential nutrients that cats cannot obtain from a balanced, meat-based diet. Regularly giving dairy to cats can also contribute to unnecessary weight gain and associated health issues, due to its high calorie and fat content.
