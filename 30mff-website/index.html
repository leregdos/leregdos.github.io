Finding Campus Cats

An honest exposé of our feline friends on campus

Story Overview

Given a short timeframe of 30 minutes, we embarked on a delightful mission to discover as many of our cherished furry friends as possible. The adventure was nothing short of exciting, as each minute brought us closer to some of the most charming and fluffy characters on NYUAD campus. Our hearts are now set on promoting the protection of these lovable creatures through the short film we made.
